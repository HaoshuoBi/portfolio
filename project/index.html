Friends Data Analysis Project

  • Data Cleaning: Processed TV show data of Friends (ratings, audience reviews, and scores) using Python. Handled missing and abnormal values, and generated descriptive statistics such as director experience distributions and rating trends.
  • Statistical Analysis: Applied confidence intervals and hypothesis testing to compare episode ratings across major directors. Evaluated statistical significance to reveal how directing style influenced show quality.
  • Clustering Analysis: Conducted k-means clustering on character dialogue sentiment. Identified emotional differences across characters and produced a final report with recommendations for optimizing show production.
